# Hello World

Every developer's first blog post is a *hello world*, so here we are.

## Why this site?

I built The Doctor's Corner for a few reasons:

1. **To document my learning** — writing forces you to actually understand something.
2. **To practice web dev** — this site *is* the project. Building it teaches me Svelte, Tailwind, and whatever else I throw at it.
3. **To have a corner of the internet that's mine** — no algorithm, no feed, just posts.

## What to expect

I'll be writing about whatever I'm working on:

- Projects I'm building
- Tools and libraries I'm learning
- Mistakes I made (and how I fixed them)
- Opinions on tech I have no business having opinions about

It won't always be polished. That's the point.

Stick around. It gets better.
